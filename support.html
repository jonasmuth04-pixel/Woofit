Frequently Asked Questions

🐕 How do I add a new dog?

Go to Settings and tap "Add Dog". Fill in your dog's name, breed, size, and age group to get started with a personalized health plan.

🔒 Is my data safe?

Yes. All your data is stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. We don't operate our own servers and never have access to your personal data.

👨‍👩‍👦 Can I share my dog's profile with family?

Absolutely! In Settings, tap "Share" next to your dog's name to generate a code. The other person can join by tapping "Join a Shared Dog" in their Settings.

💳 How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Manage Subscription. You can also manage it through the App Store under your Apple ID settings.

🗑️ How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual dogs from Settings by swiping left. Uninstalling the App removes all local data. iCloud data is removed when you disable sync.
