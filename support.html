Frequently Asked Questions

How do I add a new dog?

Go to Settings and tap "Add Dog". Fill in your dog's name, breed, date of birth, and weight to get started.

Is my data safe?

Yes. All your data is stored in your private iCloud account using Apple's CloudKit. We don't operate our own servers and never have access to your personal data.

Can I share my dog's profile with family members?

Absolutely! In Settings, tap "Share" next to your dog's name. The other person can join by tapping "Join a Shared Dog" in their Settings.

How do I manage my subscription?

Go to Settings > Manage Subscription. You can also manage it through the App Store under your Apple ID settings.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete individual dogs from Settings by swiping left, or use "Delete All Data" in Settings > Data to remove everything.
